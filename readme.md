# 🚀 Despioneer

> Small game developed during the event organized by [UndesJeux](https://www.twitch.tv/undesjeux) for the 4th anniversary of his Twitch channel.

On March 3, 1972, NASA launched the Pioneer 10 space probe, carrying a plaque intended for extraterrestrial life forms...  
**But were our intentions really as peaceful as they seemed...?**

---

## 🎯 Our 4 Constraints

| Constraint | Description |
|---|---|
| 📚 **You learn stuff** | Click on the different exoplanets to learn more about them |
| ⚡ **Speedrun (hidden)** | One action in the game allows you to complete the level instantly |
| 👿 **The villain is the main character** | In our game, it's the human race that attacks defenseless aliens |
| 🎨 **One environment = One art style** | Each planet is drawn by and in the style of a different team member |
